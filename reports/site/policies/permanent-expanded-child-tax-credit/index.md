# Permanent Expanded Child Tax Credit

> Make the 2021 expanded CTC ($3,600/child) permanent and fully refundable.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|

| Health impact | **+14 months** HALE |

| Action | implement |
| Category | income_security |

**Current status:** Reverted to $2,000/child, not fully refundable (excludes poorest families)

**Target:** $3,600/child under 6, $3,000 ages 6-17, fully refundable

## Evidence

2021 expanded CTC cut child poverty by 46% in 6 months — largest single-year reduction ever. Canada's similar program reduced child poverty by 30%. Cost: ~$100B/yr.
