# Carbon Fee-and-Dividend

> Tax carbon emissions, return 100% of revenue as equal citizen dividend. Based on British Columbia model.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Income impact | **+$-1,191/yr** (-2% of median) |
| Health impact | **+18 months** HALE |
| Evidence grade | **B** |
| Causal confidence | 66% |
| Action | implement |
| Category | environment |

**Current status:** No federal carbon price; 12 states have cap-and-trade or carbon tax

**Target:** $50/ton starting price, rising $10/yr, 100% returned as dividend

## Evidence

British Columbia: emissions down 5-15% vs control, GDP growth maintained. Switzerland carbon tax reduced emissions 20%. Dividend protects low-income households.
