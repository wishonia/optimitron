    (3) **AMERICAN MEDIFUND (AMF):** A government-funded safety net for low-income citizens who cannot cover their out-of-pocket expenses even with AMA and AML. Eligibility for AMF assistance will be determined through a transparent means-testing process, ensuring no citizen is denied necessary basic healthcare due to financial constraints [13](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQF86l7m7lgpK9iKzF5TL66USOQ2rPbYj77zvWsyjniMYVlN2C6EJGtoXs2yE9Vrl9It3ly0UIWDf8Ty_Wau7xXN73HZc6SpqbI_3Ebn0msj_LLYsRk2dfYRoH3FkdFCyGo9znBWoH4qAjow6lvFma5YPDS_5kxUmwjBZp-Fn6DSAIjQmgMM0NK7zix-d68lURJIuw==) [14](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFByrW0ACwkPA1vLQmd650JX7O2USQao10X8e7g6af6Rshu5VNdKh9Kqp6OSD0m_BSmxnN0aYDM_cWz7Ye1W1WpxJ6wTLLyk4DjFH8B3oOlLd6R_T2Mv-00mY5ZX8MZYgadmMrKx-xRIEVKR0r-KlalzZjlw_fT53Z24h2pC2SVYnZhWfOGBzFFqok8C_EJvmIJnh4RLqQZROoZEQ==) [15](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQHIq05j-_o3G6wjDCU9ECG3zWKZvj_HV7Aj7rsX_UO1Zs7OslgtSK5QEi9eHafdWu8uLY39KQ0DhNCr5nxkHqGQln4JtGu2yG7HQ_5jqChgplJko6rd8In7X9CMVdX5lkJzGONL2V7jGL2umNL31vcdXrPjNbRAUmW57oJz8yjADRAa8Cs47c8b_fZy_XDWJNd3K8k3vg==) [16](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQEujJp4nJSByLjAb1GFGYTHUMNFdPwsy0_f-EnVyZkcLZeIaAUNoncuAH96NG_daAgKmkzGLQtQ_OztofN_E4gsOX9dx_7bLFqb--Bbz7moSlZqojXuh0b-fF20KkH7SbNi6TeZcbJQEj2C2woVjmW4jXZSagZHxC-euemzgPoUhaid6GjLuTvwDWzbvwaO7CDj1cUfQoyul7Y=) [17](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQElfOb48rLbN12aAzvjhBEzIB_f3_6fOnTxnziXJD04_n0FqNGqzpLyWGdBysvQvW4mlHWf6uae5vTZiFJrofJA9LDLf3TMlqh6V78nOXoNHzDngbTjcgKNjN4X-gDBwsVWMmggiUmBV9HGHGgNCdqTn6PxEDTadiXRCO9O9-hRYnpZBUbZt3f1MrH6pk8MxT8orn7A8hI-cX8PCQJQjRsm7Hz3IlBQXQ==).

(a) **WHAT IT DOES (MODELED ON THE UK'S NIHR):** This section establishes the National Institute for Evidence-Based Research (NIER), a federal entity dedicated to funding and facilitating pragmatic clinical trials that produce actionable evidence at a fraction of the current cost, directly inspired by the UK's National Institute for Health and Care Research (NIHR) model [10](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFxIj1IK9Ga1QzDt7JI0GkP0I6CV7M4triybvB0Ycpqto2ox1iEJj6WoCbY0pFCf_s10YoDfD-DXFFP19UZERNxbiQLydSF2S0m7xjDHeVA4gUbcBKY8Nbfvy8_07jlV0m--m8vFg==) [23](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQH1LbSfmKHWwX1es6__TmAvhmm6_87AWJzugGWJqdg7PBLG9UcyboDW0tzuLPpTir9Ya23MquJaHwymg7_-yiTirq-bsCpxSXWlU9CnLwjBU4IoGTEiabDuBVFhW6RPPHWiZZKR3_5IRDhcVfI=) [24](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQHpCyiUnLfevCKjbghzvUPApbOfsT2z0dqlO7R8JoisKssNclmGJkRg8PluGSqJfVlFabRe99uhAdJTxrlt_n6vG7ZADGxY7O3B9gn7uktUPeQhgzN4HxQsWj-6lg_L_7O88BgfXP8fyZdGAEOkjWqvrBtGXITK187MBCCCvL9LRxrs9Ce4kvaD) [25](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFpD1nrHXzEkEfYloAOpOyqDrtWKtgr5ybbh9wGHz4ft89Qe-gn5KcjLQDJM3r9Nu1AnneK3COY0ML_yzdZQbVnhKfBM7A4jDBw23gvPTWQwjuYG-8CYv4G-x1pq5yddgVzioFOgi3LwX9MJN4=). NIER will prioritize research questions relevant to improving health outcomes and health system efficiency, embedding research within routine clinical practice.

### SECTION 6. OPTIMIZATION DIVIDEND POOL AND CITIZEN CHOICE.

(a) **ESTABLISHMENT OF THE OPTIMIZATION DIVIDEND POOL:** All net savings generated from the provisions of this Act shall be consolidated into a single "Optimization Dividend Pool" within the U.S. Treasury.

(b) **DISTRIBUTION OF OPTIMIZATION DIVIDEND:** The total annual savings from the Optimization Dividend Pool shall be distributed equally to every adult citizen of the United States as a monthly payment. With an estimated adult U.S. population of 258,000,000, the projected annual dividend per adult citizen is approximately **$11,378**, translating to a monthly payment of approximately **$948.16.**

(c) **CITIZEN CHOICE PROVISION:**
    (1) **VOLUNTARY REDIRECTION:** Every citizen receiving the Optimization Dividend shall have the option to redirect any portion of their monthly payment to any federal budget category of their choosing (e.g., Defense, Healthcare, Education, Infrastructure, etc.).
    (2) **DEFAULT OPTION:** The default option shall be to receive the full cash payment of the Optimization Dividend.
    (3) **IMPLEMENTATION MECHANISM:** The Department of the Treasury, in coordination with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS), shall establish an online portal (similar to the Presidential Election Campaign Fund checkbox on tax returns) through which citizens can easily opt-in to redirect funds and modify their allocation quarterly. This mechanism allows for revealed preference in government spending, demonstrating public priorities through voluntary financial allocation.

### SECTION 8. EVALUATION & SUNSET PROVISIONS.

**SEC. 801. BASELINE MEASUREMENT.**

Within 90 days of enactment of this Act, the Government Accountability Office (GAO) shall establish and publicly publish baseline measurements for the following metrics:
    (a) **Real After-Tax Median Household Income:** Derived from data collected by the U.S. Census Bureau and the Bureau of Labor Statistics.
    (b) **Median Healthy Life Expectancy at Birth (HALE):** Derived from data collected by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and the National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS).

**SEC. 802. REVIEW CADENCE.**

The GAO shall conduct a biennial review of this Act, re-measuring both metrics specified in Section 801 and comparing them against the established baselines. The results of each review shall be published publicly.

**SEC. 803. TARGET THRESHOLDS.**

The following target thresholds for improvement shall be established:
    (a) **Real After-Tax Median Household Income:** A sustained annual growth rate of at least +0.5 percentage points per year in real after-tax median household income, aiming for a cumulative increase of +2.5 percentage points within five years of full implementation.
    (b) **Median Healthy Life Expectancy at Birth:** A sustained annual increase of at least +0.3 years in median healthy life expectancy at birth, aiming for a cumulative increase of +1.5 years within five years of full implementation.
    (c) **Minimum Acceptable Thresholds:** Failure to meet 50% of the target thresholds for BOTH metrics shall constitute a failure to achieve the minimum acceptable outcomes of this Act.

**SEC. 804. HARD SUNSET.**

If, by the second biennial review (Year 4 following enactment), **BOTH** the real after-tax median household income and the median healthy life expectancy at birth fail to meet their respective minimum acceptable thresholds as defined in Section 803(c), this Act shall automatically expire and be repealed, effective 180 days after the publication of the second biennial review, unless renewed by a supermajority vote (two-thirds majority in both chambers) of Congress.

**SEC. 805. DATA COLLECTION MANDATE.**

To ensure accurate and ongoing evaluation, the following agencies are mandated to collect, track, and publicly publish the necessary data:
    (a) **Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) and U.S. Census Bureau:** Shall annually collect and publish data on real after-tax median household income.
    (b) **Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS):** Shall annually collect and publish data on median healthy life expectancy at birth.
    (c) **Office of Management and Budget (OMB):** Shall annually publish detailed per-capita spending data for federal research and development and healthcare expenditures, disaggregated by program and agency.
    (d) **Accessibility:** All mandated data shall be made publicly accessible through user-friendly online databases.

**SEC. 806. BENCHMARK JURISDICTIONS.**

The GAO shall, in its biennial reviews, explicitly benchmark the performance of the United States against the following OECD countries: The Netherlands, Norway, Denmark, and Singapore. If these benchmark countries continue to outperform the United States on both after-tax median household income and median healthy life expectancy at birth, despite the reforms enacted by this Act, the review shall include a detailed analysis explaining the persistent disparity and recommending specific legislative and programmatic adjustments to address it.
